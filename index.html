Introduction

SheSync is a women's health and wellness application designed to help users manage their menstrual health more effectively. The app allows users to track their menstrual cycles, monitor their daily moods, record personal experiences through a diary feature, and access self-care resources to support their physical and emotional well-being.


In addition, SheSync helps users locate nearby clinics and view community-shared healthcare information, making it easier to seek medical assistance when needed. By providing these features in a single platform, SheSync aims to promote better health awareness, encourage self-care, and empower women to take a proactive role in managing their overall well-being.
